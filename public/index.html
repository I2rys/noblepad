Why

Secure

NoblePad is created with a high sense of security ensuring everything is encrypted, and privacy-focused providers are utilized AMAP.

Private

NoblePad is zero-knowledge by design. We don't know what you store, and we will never know. The code is 100% open-source for you to see.

Anonymous

You don't need to register to use NoblePad, simply visit the website and input your pad. That's how simple and private it is.

 

Simple Policy

  1. Always free to use. Huge thank you to Vexhub for providing the database.
  2. Everything is encrypted, yes everything. We don't know anything.
  3. We don't care what you store because we can't know nor check. Also It's open-source.
  4. You can delete your pad anytime and anywhere.
  5. We are not responsible if you lost your pad, or someone somehow got accessed to your pad.
  6. The website does not log any information.
  7. Only your hashed primary key, code, key, location, and encrypted content is stored in the database. Nothing else.
